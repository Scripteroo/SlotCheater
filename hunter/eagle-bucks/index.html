üîç How MHB Progressives Work

Jackpot starts at floor ($9,000) and MUST hit by ceiling ($10,000).

Meter rises on coin-in. Typical: $250-$667 in = $1 meter rise.

Hits at random point between current and ceiling (uniform for Ainsworth).

If expected return > expected cost, you have +EV!
